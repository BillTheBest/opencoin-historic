SUM
-----

A:
  SUM_ANNOUNCE  #base64(transaction_id)
                standard identifier
                currency identifier
                amount
                target (what exactly is the target? Should it be base64 encoded?)
        Prior:  Hello
        Follow: SUM_ACCEPT 
                SUM_REJECT

B:
  SUM_ACCEPT
        Prior:  SUM_ANNOUNCE
        Follow: TOKEN_REDEEM
                        
  SUM_REJECT
        Prior:  SUM_ANNOUNCE
        Follow: Goodbye
                        


TOKEN
-----

A:
  TOKEN_REDEEM  list(token)
        Prior:  SUM_ACCEPT
        Follow: TOKEN_REJECT
                TOKEN_ACCEPT

B:
  TOKEN_REJECT  type
                list(detail-reason)|list()
                    Reasons:
                        'Rejected'
                        'Invalid token'
                        'See detail':
                            'None'
                            All options of Reason, except 'See detail'
        Prior:  TOKEN_REDEEM
        Follow: TOKEN_REDEEM
                Goodbye

  TOKEN_ACCEPT
        Prior:  TOKEN_REDEEM
        Follow: Goodbye
